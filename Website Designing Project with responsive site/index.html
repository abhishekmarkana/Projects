Brewed Coffee

A fresh brew highlights coffee's rich, complex flavors.

Coffee is a brewed drink made from roasted coffee beans, which are the seeds of berries from the Coffea plant. Native to tropical regions of Africa, coffee spread globally and is now cultivated in several countries. Each region produces coffee with unique flavors, influenced by climate and soil conditions.

The process of making coffee begins with harvesting ripe coffee cherries, followed by processing and drying the beans. After roasting, the beans release essential oils, creating the familiar rich aroma. Finally, these beans are ground and brewed to produce the beloved beverage enjoyed worldwide.

Our Journey

1971 - The First Starbucks

Starbucks opened its first store in Seattle's Pike Place Market, selling high-quality coffee beans and equipment.

1990s - Going Global

Starbucks expanded internationally, bringing their unique coffeehouse experience to new communities worldwide.


Our Commitment to Sustainability

Starbucks is dedicated to ethical sourcing and building sustainable partnerships with farmers around the world. By investing in communities and supporting sustainable farming practices, we help ensure a high-quality coffee future for generations to come.

We are committed to reducing our environmental footprint, promoting recycling and minimizing waste wherever possible.
